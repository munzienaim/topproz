TAKE CONTROL OF
YOUR STAFF & TIME.

A software built with all dilemmas in mind- we have engineered all of the possible functionalities necessary to manage a large scale service business. Take control of your team's time and shape with staff tools to fit your business.

MANAGE YOUR TEAM

create your staff tree and get a grip on your employees. map out everyone’s role in your business- right into the software. add or remove employees, change statuses (in case of vacation, breaks, etc) and get a clean view of who's in, and who’s out.

MEET YOUR STAFF'S NEEDS

upon welcoming a staff member into your business, you can configure their own unique schedule, commissions, pay rate, and all other relevant data.

KEEP EMPLOYEES IN SYNC

your employees will now need to manual clock-in on their own device to keep track of their time. notifications can be set to remind employees to clock in and clock out, take breaks, and to adjourn to a job site or depart. employees will no longer be paid for cushioned hours or idle time wasted outside of a job site.
